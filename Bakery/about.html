About The Simple Bake Shop

Our Story

The Simple Bake Shop began as a small neighborhood kitchen with one goal: to bring warm, classic, freshly baked goods to our community. What started as a weekend hobby slowly became a daily passion, and eventually, a cozy bakery filled with the smell of bread, butter, and cinnamon.

We focus on simple recipes, real ingredients, and traditional baking methods. Every loaf, pastry, and sweet treat is made by hand with care.

Our Mission

“To create comforting, everyday baked goods that feel like home.”

We believe in slow fermentation, honest ingredients, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. We want every customer to feel cared for, whether they're picking up a loaf of sourdough or enjoying a morning pastry with a cup of coffee.

What Makes Us Different

✔ Freshly baked every single morning

✔ Simple, honest ingredients, nothing artificial

✔ Warm, charming atmosphere

✔ A menu inspired by timeless bakery classics
