Frequently Asked Questions

Do you bake everything fresh daily?

Yes! All breads, pastries, cookies, and sweets are baked fresh every morning.

Do you take custom cake orders?

We offer seasonal cakes by the slice, but do not take custom cake orders at this time.

What time should I come for the freshest items?

We recommend stopping by between 7 AM and 10 AM for the best selection of breads and pastries.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

We currently do not offer gluten-free baked goods, but we're working on recipes!

Can I call ahead to reserve items?

Yes! Give us a call in the morning and we'll gladly set aside items for you.

Where are you located?

We are located at 456 Main Street, Townsville, CA 12345.
